Help:Contents

Guidance for using the Aetherpedia.

Navigating Aetherpedia

The primary method of navigation is the central search bar. You can either use the live search dropdown that appears as you type, or you can press Enter or click the magnifying glass to go to a dedicated search results page.

Searching & Importing

The Search Page shows results from both Aetherpedia and Wikipedia. For Wikipedia results, you can click the "Import" button to create a local copy of that article in the Aetherpedia. This allows for community additions and archival.

Updating Articles

If an Aetherpedia article was originally imported from Wikipedia, an "Update from Wikipedia" button will appear in the top tab bar. Clicking this will fetch the latest version of the article, overwriting the existing local copy. This is useful for keeping our archives current.
